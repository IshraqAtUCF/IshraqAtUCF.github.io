About

I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Engineering at the University of Central Florida. My work focuses on building practical, automation-driven security verification flows for modern SoCs, with an emphasis on LLM-guided threat modeling, assertion generation, and formal proof.

I also work on chiplet/System-in-Package authentication and secure hardware design, combining hardware security ideas with verification and systems thinking.

Current Focus

  • ATLAS: AI-assisted threat-to-assertion learning for SoC security verification
  • System-level harness generation and security property quality checking
  • InterPUF: distributed chiplet/interposer authentication using PUF + MPC
  • Formal verification workflows with JasperGold and static/AST-driven reasoning
